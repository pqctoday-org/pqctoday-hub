Forward Together: Solving IAM as a Community

Growth & Innovation

From evolving standards to emerging tech, IAM for research and education is changing fast. That’s why InCommon brings together working groups, industry experts, and peers to explore new solutions and build what’s next — together. Through events, forums, and the InCommon Academy, members collaborate, learn, and lead the way forward.

Inter-Organizational Collaboration

InCommon provides a space for meaningful connection. Institutions across the U.S. and beyond come together to share ideas, mentorship, and solutions that scale. By working across boundaries, members strengthen both their local systems and our collective infrastructure.

Where Learners Become Leaders

The InCommon Academy brings IT professionals together to learn, grow, and lead in identity and access management. From hands-on software training to real-world problem-solving, our programs equip your team with the skills, tools, and guidance needed to build scalable IAM solutions and strengthen inter-organizational collaboration.

The Tools Behind Trustworthy Federation

We offer a suite of proven, open-source IAM tools tailored to the needs of R&E. These solutions help institutions manage digital identities, streamline account provisioning, enforce role-based access policies, and enable secure single sign-on, all while integrating within complex, multi-campus environments. The result is more control, less friction, and future-ready scalability.

Learn More
Secure Global Wireless Access with eduroam

We provide fast, reliable, and secure wireless access to students and researchers across hundreds of partner campuses worldwide. Whether they’re at home or visiting another institution, users can sign in once and stay securely connected.

Learn More
Simplified Encryption for Trusted Collaboration

Protecting research data, institutional records, and user privacy is critical. Our certificates service help institutions secure web traffic, applications, and APIs through trusted digital certificates. Through this service, we support long-term data integrity and compliance with security and privacy standards across a connected academic ecosystem.
