About Quantum Protect

Quantum Protect extends the u.trust General Purpose HSM Se-Series with proven and standardized Post Quantum Cryptography algorithms for a secure transition into the quantum era, featuring robust lattice-based and hash-based algorithms. For upfront evaluation and testing, a free simulator is available.

Supported Platform: u.trust General Purpose HSM Se-Series

  • Key Features

  • Description

  • Specifications

Post Quantum Cryptography (PQC) Algorithms

Offers standardized and mature PQC algorithms for signature creation and key encapsulation for a secure and seamless transition to quantum-security.

Free PQC Simulator

Use the free, market-unique Quantum Protect simulator to evaluate how PQC algorithms work within your environment and use case.

Crypto-agile in-field upgrade

Quantum Protect is available as seamless in-field upgrade for the u.trust General Purpose HSM Se-Series – no HSM exchange needed.

Quantum Protect is the application package that adds Post Quantum Cryptography to the u.trust General Purpose HSM Se-Series. It offers the lattice-based algorithms ML-KEM and ML-DSA and the stateful hash-based signature schemes LMS and XMSS (incl. their multitree variants HSS and XMSS-MT). More algorithms such as SLH-DSA are on the roadmap.

The solution is designed crypto-agile, so Quantum Protect can be added to the HSM via an in-field upgrade – no HSM exchange needed. The free simulator allows integration and evaluation testing of algorithms before deployment.

Cryptographic Interfaces (APIs)

  • PQC Algorithms

  • Easy integration

PQC Algorithms

  • ML-KEM (FIPS 203)
  • ML-DSA (FIPS 204)
  • LMS and HSS
  • XMSS and XMSS-MT
  • SLH-DSA (FIPS 205) in progress

Easy integration

  • Application integration using PKCS #11 “Vendor Defined Mechanisms”
  • Firmware module for in-field upgrade on your Hardware Security Module
  • Free, downloadable simulator for evaluation, development, and integration testing
