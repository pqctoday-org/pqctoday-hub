Industry Insights

The cryptography landscape is evolving fast, from global standardisation efforts to new regulatory and implementation challenges. PQShield’s Industry Insights bring together expert analysis, guidance, and commentary on the latest developments shaping the future of post-quantum security.

Below is a graphical summary of the PQC window for deployment, followed by our analysis of key developments across standards, research, and real-world implementation. We break down what each update means in practice, cutting through complexity to highlight the implications for organisations planning their transition.

This is a continuously updated resource designed to help you stay ahead , whether you are tracking developments, shaping strategy, or actively preparing for a quantum-secure future.

For more information about country and government specific guidance, head over to our Roadmaps & Transition Guidance page.
